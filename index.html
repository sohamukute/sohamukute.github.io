About Me

Hello! I'm Soham Kute, an Electronics Engineering student at Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute (VJTI) in Mumbai with a minor in Aerospace Engineering. My passion lies at the intersection of electronics, robotics, and aerospace technologies.

Currently pursuing my B.Tech degree (2023-2027), I focus on developing innovative solutions to complex engineering problems. My academic journey, which began at St. Lawrence High School and Junior College in Nashik (where I achieved 93.4% in 10th grade and 84% in 12th grade), has equipped me with a strong foundation in mathematics and physics.

I'm particularly interested in control systems, embedded systems development, and autonomous robotics. When I'm not coding or soldering, you can find me exploring new technologies or contributing to projects at the Society of Robotics and Automation (SRA) at VJTI.

I believe in continuous learning and am always eager to connect with like-minded individuals who share my passion for technology and innovation. Whether it's collaborating on a project or discussing emerging tech trends, I'm always open to new opportunities and connections!
